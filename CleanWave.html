Our Mission

CleanWave's mission is to create a community-driven platform that empowers individuals to actively participate in ocean cleanup efforts. Through this platform, users can upload videos of themselves cleaning the oceans and other water bodies, and they earn points for their contributions. These points can be redeemed to obtain free items from the CleanWave store.

How does it work?

When you're ready to start making a difference on CleanWave, it's time to roll up your sleeves and capture the essence of your ocean cleanup activities in the most compelling way possible. This is your opportunity to not only clean up our beautiful beaches, shorelines, and water bodies but also to inspire and engage a global community of like-minded individuals. Ensure that your videos are clear, well-documented, and thoughtfully edited to effectively demonstrate your contribution. Highlight the extent of the pollution you're addressing, the magnitude of waste collected, and the positive transformation you're bringing to the environment. Share your passion and dedication, as well as any unique techniques or insights you have developed in your cleanup efforts. By doing so, you're not only contributing to the CleanWave mission but also motivating others to take part and collectively make a lasting impact on the health and sustainability of our oceans. Your videos will serve as a powerful testament to the difference every individual can make in the fight against ocean pollution and the preservation of our planet's natural beauty.

FAQ

What is CleanWave?

CleanWave is a community-driven platform dedicated to ocean cleanup efforts. We empower individuals to actively participate in cleaning the oceans and water bodies, making a positive impact on the environment.

How can I get involved in ocean cleanup with CleanWave?

Getting involved is easy! You can record videos of your cleanup activities and share them on CleanWave. Earn points for your contributions and make a difference.

What do I earn by participating in CleanWave?

You can earn points for your contributions, which can be redeemed for free items from the CleanWave store. It's our way of showing appreciation for your efforts.

How do I upload videos to CleanWave?

To upload videos, log in to your CleanWave account and visit your profile. You'll find an option to upload your cleanup videos there. Follow the provided instructions for uploading and sharing.
